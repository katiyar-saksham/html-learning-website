üéØ Media Best Practices - Your Success Guide

üöÄ Performance Tips:
  • Optimize file sizes - Compress images and videos for faster loading
  • Use lazy loading - Add loading="lazy" to images
  • Choose the right format - WebP for images, MP4 for videos
  • Provide fallbacks - Multiple sources for better compatibility
‚ôø Accessibility Champions:
  • Always use alt text - Describe what people see
  • Add captions - Use <track> for video subtitles
  • Don't autoplay with sound - Respect your users' ears!
  • Provide transcripts - Text versions of audio content
üì± Mobile-Friendly Media:
  • Test on phones - Make sure everything works on small screens
  • Consider data usage - Mobile users often have limited data
  • Touch-friendly controls - Make buttons big enough for fingers
  • Responsive sizing - Use percentages instead of fixed pixels

Remember: Great media doesn't just look good - it works for everyone, loads fast, and enhances your story! üåü
