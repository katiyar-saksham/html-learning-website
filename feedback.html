Pro Tips for Creating Great Forms:

  • Always use <label>: It's essential for accessibility and user experience.
  • Group with <fieldset>: It makes long forms much easier to read.
  • Choose the right <input> type: Use type="email" for emails, type="number" for numbers, etc. It helps with validation, especially on mobile!
  • Use placeholder text: Guide your users on what to enter.
  • Don't ask for too much: Only ask for the information you truly need. A shorter form is more likely to be completed.
